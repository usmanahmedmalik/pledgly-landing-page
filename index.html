Running a mosque shouldn't mean drowning in admin

Most mosque boards are volunteers — imams, engineers, teachers — giving their evenings and weekends to keep the community running. They deserve better tools.

Donation tracking in Excel

Multiple volunteers updating different spreadsheets. No one knows the real total. Ramadan ends and reconciliation takes weeks.

WhatsApp groups doing everything

Announcements, payment confirmations, course schedules — all buried in 300 messages. Volunteers burn out managing it.

Quran school on paper registers

Student lists, attendance, homework — manually maintained. Parents have no visibility. Teachers have no tools.

Everything You Need to Manage Your Mosque

Streamline donations, courses, and community engagement with purpose-built tools.

Annual Pledge Management

Members commit to a yearly amount and pay in installments. Pledgly tracks who's paid and sends gentle reminders — no awkward conversations needed.

Online & Recurring Donations

Secure Stripe-powered payments. Members give once or set up a monthly contribution. Every transaction is logged automatically — no manual reconciliation.

Quran School & Education

Manage class schedules, student enrollment, teacher portals, attendance, and homework. Parents log in to check progress.

Member Self-Service Portal

Every member gets their own login to see pledge history, make payments, enroll in courses, and update info — reducing admin requests.

Admin Dashboard & Reports

A complete overview of your mosque's finances, membership, and programs. Export reports for your annual general meeting in minutes.
